About

SEO is what I do best

I'm a Digital Marketing Manager who specializes in SEO and organic growth. Over the past 6+ years, I've helped SaaS, IT services, and B2B companies improve their search visibility, traffic, and content performance.

My core strength is the technical and strategic side of search — SEO audits, Core Web Vitals, schema and structured data, site architecture, keyword research, search intent mapping, content strategy, and link building. I work closely with developers, content teams, and leadership to turn SEO plans into shipped results.

At Binmile, I scaled organic traffic 3X and lifted Domain Authority from 18 to 40. I also worked directly with a UK-based financial services client, which gave me hands-on international SEO experience. Today I lead SEO and a small content team at an IT services company, and I'm building practical experience in AI search optimization (AEO/GEO) and Google AI Overviews — because I think that's where organic visibility is heading next.
